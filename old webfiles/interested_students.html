I'm looking for motivated and energetic students to work together on problems in security, privacy, human-computer interaction (HCI) and ubiquitous computing. Experience in other areas including machine learning, systems, signal processing, ubiquitous computing and theory is also a plus. If you are interested, you can Email me.
