Openings

I'm looking for motivated and energetic students to work together on problems in security and privacy. Prior experience in security or privacy is not mandatory. However, candidates should be eager to learn and explore new things. Experience in other areas including HCI, (adversarial) machine learning, natural language processing, reverse engineering, mobile-operating systems, signal processing, network protocols (e.g., ZeeBee, ZWave, Bluethooth) or web developement is a plus. If you are interested, you can Email me.

NCSU is a public research university (R1) located in beautiful Raleigh at North Carolina, ranked as one of the best places to live in the USA. It is a part of the well-known research triangle (NCSU, Duke and UNC). The region's growing high-technology community includes companies such as IBM, SAS Institute, Cisco Systems, NetApp, Red Hat, EMC Corporation; so, a lot of internship opportunities in summer. So consider applying to NCSU for Fall 2021!
